My Top 5 Fantasy Romances


1. Caraval (series)

Stephanie Garber

This series has three books: Caraval, Legendary, and Finale. This series made me realize how much I love books that just feel magical. Throughout these three books, particularly Caraval, I could just feel the magic in this world. I loved going through the book and figuring out the mysteries when the main character did. Because of the magical elements, it was difficult to predict what would happen next. I also thouroughly enjoyed the romance aspect of the series. The relationships of the two couples were throughout all three of the books and I appreciated how they impacted the plot. I also loved how the author created another book series in the same world with some of the same characters because I got to see what happened to them after the trilogy ended. This series will always be one of my favorites and so I highly recommend it.

5. Heaven Official's Blessing (series)

Mo Xiang Tong Xiu

When I got these books from the library, I didn't even really know what it was about. All I knew was that I heard about it somewhere online and that it was a Chinese fantasy novel. Little did I know was that I was about to read the first five books in a couple of weeks. I wasn't completely sure of it at first because the first novel was a bit slow, but then it picked up right away. The plot was so complex and it dove into the characters' backgrounds and emotions so well. This is a fantasy romance book, so the romance was one of the biggest parts that made me love this series so much. The relationship was somewhat slowburn and it was just so fun to read. There were so many parts that made me laugh or cry, and I really just loved reading this series.
